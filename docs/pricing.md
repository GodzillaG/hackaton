# Pricing rationale

ScolioScan School uses three access levels:

| Tier | Price | Monthly resources | Target user |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | $0 | 10 Basic, 0 Advanced | quick individual checks |
| Plus | $19/month | 80 Basic, 8 Advanced | families and regular personal monitoring |
| Corporate | custom | negotiated with school | schools, districts, medical offices |

Rationale:

- Free keeps the product accessible for first checks and demos.
- Plus is priced below clinician education platforms while still high enough to support AI compute and storage.
- Corporate is quote-based because school health and employer MSK products are usually sold by organization size, rollout scope, and support level.

Market references checked:

- Medbridge public pricing lists annual individual plans at $100 for students and $275-$325 for clinician-focused plans, with large groups directed to sales.
- Hinge Health positions MSK care for employers and health plans through demo/contact flows rather than fixed public self-serve pricing.
- Frontline School Health Management is a K-12 health workflow product focused on school nurses and district operations, also sold as an organizational solution.
